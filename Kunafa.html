Kunafa



Instructions:

1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. Untangle the kataifi strands using your fingers.
3. Place the kataifi in a large bowl and pour the melted butter over it. Toss until the kataifi is evenly coated.
4. Press half of the kataifi mixture into the bottom of a greased 9x13 inch baking pan to create a crust.
5. Sprinkle the finely chopped nuts evenly over the kataifi crust.
6. Cover the nuts with the remaining kataifi mixture, pressing it down gently.
7. Bake in the preheated oven for about 40-45 minutes or until golden brown.
8. While the kunafa is baking, prepare the sugar syrup. In a saucepan, combine water, sugar, and lemon juice. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 10-15 minutes until it thickens slightly. Add orange blossom water if using.
9. When the kunafa is done baking, pour the ghee or clarified butter evenly over the top.
10. Pour the prepared sugar syrup evenly over the hot kunafa. Allow it to soak and cool for at least 2 hours.
11. Cut the kunafa into squares or diamonds and serve. Enjoy your delightful homemade kunafa !

Ingredients:
- 1 pound kataifi (shredded phyllo dough)
- 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
- 2 cups finely chopped unsalted pistachios or your preferred nuts
- 1 cup ghee or clarified butter
- 2 cups water
- 2 cups granulated sugar
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon orange blossom water (optional)
- 1 cup simple syrup (1 cup sugar + 1/2 cup water, boiled until sugar dissolves)
