Ratatouille



Instructions:

1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
3. Add chopped onions and minced garlic. Sauté until softened.
4. Add diced eggplant, sliced zucchini, and diced bell peppers. Cook for about 5-7 minutes until the vegetables start to soften.
5. Stir in diced tomatoes and tomato paste. Cook for an additional 5 minutes.
6. Add chopped basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
7. Transfer the mixture to a baking dish, spreading it evenly.
8. Drizzle a little more olive oil over the top.
9. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
10. Garnish with additional fresh basil before serving.
Enjoy your homemade ratatouille! Serve it as a side dish, over pasta, or on its own.

Ingredients:

- 1 large eggplant, diced
- 2 zucchini, sliced
- 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 4 tomatoes, diced
- 2 tablespoons tomato paste
- 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1/4 cup olive oil
