Sizzling Brownie



Instructions:

For Brownie:
1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a square baking pan.
2. In a saucepan, melt the butter. Remove from heat and stir in sugar.
3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
4. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until well combined.
6. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out with moist crumbs (not wet batter).
7. Let the brownies cool in the pan before cutting them into squares.
For Sizzling Plate:
1. Melt butter in a small pan, add sugar, and cook until it caramelizes slightly.
For Assembly:
1. Place a few brownie squares on a sizzling plate.
2. Pour the hot caramel over the brownies on the plate.
3. Top with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
4. Drizzle with chocolate sauce and sprinkle with chopped nuts if desired.
5. Serve immediately while sizzling.
Enjoy your delightful sizzling brownie!

Ingredients:
For Brownie:
- 1 cup unsalted butter
- 2 cups granulated sugar
- 4 large eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/2 cup cocoa powder
- 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
- A pinch of salt
For Sizzling Plate:
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons sugar
For Assembly:
- Vanilla ice cream
- Chocolate sauce
- Chopped nuts (optional)
