Chicken Biriyani



Instructions:

*Marinating Chicken:*
1. In a bowl, combine chicken, yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.

*Cooking Rice:*
1. Boil water in a large pot. Add soaked rice, cardamom pods, cloves, cinnamon stick, bay leaf, and salt.
2. Cook until the rice is 70-80% done. Drain the water and set aside.

*Preparing Biryani Masala:*
1. In a deep pan, heat ghee or oil. Add sliced onions and saut√© until golden brown.
2. Add cumin seeds, fennel seeds, chopped tomatoes, and biryani masala powder. Cook until the tomatoes are soft.
3. Add marinated chicken and cook until it's half done.
4. Layer half-cooked rice over the chicken. Sprinkle chopped mint and cilantro.
5. Drizzle saffron-infused milk (optional) and fried onions over the rice.

*Dum Cooking (Slow Cooking):*
1. Seal the pan with a tight-fitting lid or aluminum foil.
2. Cook on low heat for 25-30 minutes until the chicken is tender and the rice is fully cooked.

*Serve:*
1. Gently fluff the biryani with a fork, mixing the layers.
2. Serve hot, garnished with more fried onions, mint, and cilantro.

Enjoy your flavourful and aromatic Chicken Biryani!

Ingredients:

*For Marinating Chicken:*
- 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
- 1 cup yogurt
- 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
- 1 teaspoon red chili powder
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
- Salt to taste

*For Rice:*
- 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes
- 4-5 cups water for boiling rice
- 4-5 green cardamom pods
- 4-5 cloves
- 2-inch cinnamon stick
- 1 bay leaf
- Salt to taste

*For Biryani Masala:*
- 3 large onions, thinly sliced
- 3 tomatoes, chopped
- 1/2 cup fried onions (for garnish)
- 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
- 1/4 cup chopped cilantro (coriander leaves)
- 1/2 cup ghee or oil
- 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
- 1 teaspoon fennel seeds
- 2 teaspoons biryani masala powder
- 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
- A pinch of saffron strands soaked in warm milk (optional)
