Creamy Vegetable Kurma



Instructions:

1. In a blender, combine grated coconut and soaked cashew nuts. Blend into a smooth paste, adding a little water if needed. Set aside.
2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add cumin seeds and fennel seeds. Let them splutter.
3. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
4. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears.
5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they are soft and the oil separates.
6. Add coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
7. Add chopped mixed vegetables and cook for a few minutes.
8. Add the coconut-cashew paste and yogurt. Stir well to combine.
9. Add water as needed to achieve the desired consistency. Bring it to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer until the vegetables are tender.
10. Garnish with chopped cilantro.

Serve the creamy vegetable kurma hot with rice, roti, or naan. Enjoy this delicious and aromatic dish!

Ingredients:
*For Kurma:*
- 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, beans, peas, potatoes), chopped
- 1 cup onions, finely chopped
- 1 cup tomatoes, finely chopped
- 1/2 cup fresh or frozen coconut, grated
- 1/4 cup cashew nuts, soaked in warm water
- 1/4 cup yogurt
- 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
- 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
- 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
- 1 teaspoon fennel seeds
- 1 teaspoon coriander powder
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
- Salt to taste
- Chopped cilantro for garnish
